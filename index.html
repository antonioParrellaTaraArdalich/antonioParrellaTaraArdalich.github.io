Details & FAQ

Do you have a registry? Can I bring a gift?

Your presence at our wedding is the greatest gift we could ask for. Therefore, we will not be setting up a gift registry. If you were considering giving a gift, we are saving up for our dream honeymoon and home and your contribution would be greatly appreciated. We will have a wishing well on the day. However, please know that your love and support are the most meaningful gifts of all.

When should I RSVP by and how?

To help with planning, we ask that you please RSVP by the 30th of January 2024 by clicking the RSVP tab on our wedding website and filling in the form. Whether you are able to join us or unable to attend, please RSVP to let us know. If you are having trouble RSVPing, please do not hesitate to contact us. Unfortunately, if we do not receive your RSVP by the deadline noted on your formal invitation, we will have to mark you as 'not attending'. We will really miss celebrating with you, but we do need to finalise guest count for the venue and vendors. We hope you can understand!

Can I bring a plus one?

Unfortunately, due to venue capacity restrictions, we can only accommodate those who are specifically named on the envelopes of the formal invitation. Therefore, we regretfully ask our guests to please not bring a plus one. Thank you so much for your understanding!

What time should I arrive?

Help us get the party started on time! We recommend you arrive 30 minutes before the start of the ceremony to make sure everyone is seated on time.

Is there a dress code?

We would love to see our family and friends dress up with us! We are requesting garden formal attire. Think summer garden party vibes! PSA: as there are grassy areas at the venue, we do not recommend wearing stiletto heels. If you have any questions regarding the attire, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Are kids invited?

Although we adore each one of your little ones, we have decided to keep our wedding an adults-only event so that everyone can relax and enjoy the evening. This is also a safety precaution with a lake at the venue. The only exception will be the little ones in our bridal party/immediate family. We really appreciate you making arrangements ahead of time and leaving the kids at home so you can celebrate with us. Thank you!

Can I take pictures during the wedding?

We ask that guests please put away their phone/devices during the Ceremony. This is to avoid phones or devices in the background of our hired professionals' footage, which we will absolutely share with you! However, we strongly encourage that our guests take photos/videos during the reception, and they are welcome to post it on social media and tag us!

How will I get there? Is there parking on site?

As the venue is in Cuddlee Creek, Ubers may be limited. Therefore, we will organise a private bus to transport guests to the venue from a designated location and time, and back to the pick-up location at the end of the evening. When RSVPing, please indicate if you would like to be transported by the bus, and when numbers are finalised, we will be in touch with you regarding the details. If you decide to drive, there is parking at the venue.
